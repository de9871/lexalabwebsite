Our Researchers & Students

The LEXA Lab provides hands-on research training and experience for graduate and undergraduate students who are interested in supporting autistic children and their families through rigorous research.

Principal Investigator

Dr. Yanru Chen

Assistant Professor of Autism, College of Education & Human Development, Georgia State University.

Dr. Chen is an interdisciplinary autism researcher interested in understanding how to support language, literacy, and communication development in autistic children, with a particular focus on the role of executive function.
