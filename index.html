Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between complex research and everyday impact. We study the early building blocks of language and social interaction to create better support systems for children with developmental differences.

What We Study

question_mark

How do children learn to communicate?

We investigate the early precursors to language, looking at how infants use gestures, eye contact, and vocalizations to share their world with others before they even say their first words.

question_mark

What makes social learning easier for some than others?

Every child has a unique learning style. We study how differences in attention and sensory processing influence the way children interact with peers and caregivers.

question_mark

How can we support diverse development?

Our goal is to identify strengths. We look for ways to build on a child's natural abilities to help them overcome challenges and reach their full potential.
