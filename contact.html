A Note for Families

We know choosing to participate is a big decision, and we want to make it easy for you to learn more before committing to anything. We are happy to walk you through what our team does, what a visit looks like for your child, and how the research might benefit kids like yours. Reach out with any questions and we will get back to you.

For press inquiries, reprint requests, or collaboration conversations, email us directly at asdlab@gsu.edu.
